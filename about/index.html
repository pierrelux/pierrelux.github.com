Commonplacing

September 10, 2015, Pierre-Luc Bacon

I am a PhD student at McGill University in the Reasoning and Learning Lab. I spent some time during my master's thesis thinking about the so-called "options" in reinforcement learning. My main question was (and still remains): how would an agent automatically acquire a set of skills from the interaction with its environment ?

In an attempt to organize my thoughts, I sometimes try to write short notes on this blog: a modern interpretation of the commonplaces. I also have a set of IPython Notebooks available on Nbviewer.
