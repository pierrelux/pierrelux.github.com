About

I'm a PhD student working under the supervision of Doina Precup in the Reasoning and Learning Lab. I want to understand how temporally extended actions and representations thereof can be learned from experience and utilized to drive intelligent behavior. My recent work investigates the role of time in building subjective representations for planning.

You can reach me at pbacon@cs.mcgill.ca, or on Twitter as @pierrelux. I usually work in MC107, first floor of McConnell building.
